Valuable resources every new CS students can benefit from

Posted by Quan Dinh on February 24, 2021

It is important to continously learn when you are in this field. Fortunately, most resources are free and easily accessible with an internet connection. Here are the ones I recommend the most.

1.FreeCodeCamp.org

This website has thousands of free tutorials and posts that are extremely helpful. Everything is 100% free. Content is created by fellow users.

An honorable mention of a similar website is Dev.to. On this site, you can also find numerous guides and posts that are very relevant about Computer Science topics and trends.

2.The Odin Project

This is an awesome free resource to start learning about website development. The curriculum found here is extremely useful and can prepare anyone to be a good website developer.

3.CS Career Questions subreddit

This subreddit is the most relevant for anything about a career in Computer Science. You can ask and look at a ton of information that is posted. This resource is less educational and more resourceful. You can find answers to unique questions on here. For example, you can see what people who got into good internships, positions have to say about their journey here. It is also a place that people can freely discuss any obstacle or new finding about the field that they want. Be careful not to waste too much time on here. You should come here whenever you have specific questions that you think people have asked on here before.

Conclusion

There are many and many resources that one can find to navigate towards a successful career in Computer Science. It can be a YouTube channel, a Discord server, or any specific website. Feel free to explore everything that you can find. There is only one caution. Since most resources are free, be cautious with paid resources and do ask youself carefully if such resources are any better than the free ones. Have fun learning and growing!
